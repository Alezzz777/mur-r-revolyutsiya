Purr-r-revolution Manifesto

This is a revolution without barricades and without enemies. Its only weapon is warmth.

  1. Kindness is contagious. One warm act sparks the next. We pass the cat along — and pass the feeling with it.
  2. Nobody is born cruel. Most often, cruelty hides exhaustion and fear. A cat doesn't argue — it just purrs — and the person becomes softer.
  3. Small things matter more than loud ones. Feeding a stray cat, supporting someone who's struggling, not returning cruelty with cruelty — the world is built from these moments.
  4. Anyone can join. You don't need to be a hero. Just pet the cat and pass it on.
  5. Peace is when everyone pets their cats. And we're getting there one purr at a time.
